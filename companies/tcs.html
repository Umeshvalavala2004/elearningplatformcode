Preparation Tips

Expert recommendations to help you crack the TCS exam:

Time Management
  • Practice with timed mock tests to improve speed
  • Allocate time based on question difficulty
  • Don't spend too much time on any single question
  • Learn to quickly identify and skip difficult questions
Study Strategy
  • Focus on your weak areas first
  • Solve previous year question papers
  • Make formula sheets for quick revision
  • Practice daily with small sets of questions
Coding Preparation
  • Master basic data structures first
  • Practice coding on paper/whiteboard
  • Learn to write clean, efficient code
  • Understand time and space complexity
Exam Day Tips
  • Stay calm and read questions carefully
  • Attempt easy questions first
  • Double-check your answers if time permits
  • Manage stress with deep breathing
